Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 4, 2025

Script Guard is a Chrome extension that empowers developers to control, block, and manage JavaScript and CSS resources on websites they visit. We take your privacy seriously.

What Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All interactions with the extension take place locally on your device and are not shared with any servers.

Storage Permissions

Script Guard uses Chrome’s local storage only to save your preferences (such as which scripts/styles to block on a given site). This data never leaves your machine.

No User Tracking

The extension does not monitor your browsing activity, web history, or any personal information. It only interacts with the current tab when you manually activate it.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. Period.

Changes to Policy

If this policy ever changes, the new version will be published here with a revised date.
